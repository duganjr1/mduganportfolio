Development and Composition Process:
The overall process is incredibly varied dependant on what is being done and what deadlines are set. If no deadline is set then it gives me time to add details, edit, review, and then finalize. I use Maya, Blender, Unity, Unreal, Graphics Gale, DaVinci Resolve, and Mudbox. I also use OBS Studio and several IDE'S from NetBeans to Visual Studio Code. Finally, the process can be indepth, I first sketch an idea on what i'm making, prepare assets, and begin creating what I would be working on. If additional details, reference imagery, or even other assets were involved it could take less time as I'd have a foundation to work up from.
